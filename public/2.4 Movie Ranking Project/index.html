The Best Shows According to Me

My Must Watch List of All Time

Rick and Morty

Humor is super goofy and offers a lot of sci-fi adventures.

Regular Show

The most random episodes make for the funniest episodes. Anything
can happen they can be playing video games one second then 1v1ing a
god in order to save the park.

Adventure Time

Great show for action and the development of the plot and main
character is amazing.

Bob's Burgers

Goofy show that sets each episode as a slice of life genre.

Amazing World of Gumball

Super goofy show with some dumb humor, but is great for laughs.
